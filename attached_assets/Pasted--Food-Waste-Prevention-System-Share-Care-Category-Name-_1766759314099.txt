### Safety & Quality Assurance
```
FOOD SAFETY PROTOCOL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
FOR RESTAURANTS/BUSINESSES:
✅ FSSAI license verification (mandatory)
✅ Kitchen hygiene rating visible
✅ Food handler training certificates
✅ Temperature logs (hot food >60°C, cold <5°C)
✅ Packaging standards (food-grade containers)

FOR HOME SHARERS:
✅ Profile verification (ID + phone)
✅ Food safety guidelines shown
✅ "Cook & Share Responsibly" training module
✅ Disclaimer acceptance required
✅ Community ratings visible

AUTOMATIC SAFEGUARDS:
🚫 Auto-reject if >4 hours old (cooked food)
🚫 Auto-reject if damaged packaging
🚫 Flag user if 3+ complaints received
✅ Mandatory photo upload (freshness check)
✅ AI image analysis (detect spoilage)

near-expiry. AgriConnect facilitates connection
only. Inspect before consuming. Follow food
safety guidelines."
